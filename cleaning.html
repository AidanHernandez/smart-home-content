Smart cleaning in your home

Aluminum wedge

Its sleek, lightweight design allows for easy maneuverability in tight spaces, making it ideal for scraping stubborn grime and residue from surfaces without damaging them. The aluminum material is not only durable and resistant to corrosion but also effectively prevents the buildup of bacteria and mildew, ensuring a more hygienic clean.

Dishwasher

This advanced appliance features an AI-driven cleaning system that intelligently analyzes the type and level of soil on each dish, adjusting water temperature, pressure, and detergent accordingly to ensure spotless results every time. Equipped with self-cleaning filters and a high-efficiency spray arm that reaches even the most challenging angles.

Robot mice

These compact, agile devices are designed to navigate seamlessly around furniture and tight spaces, using advanced sensors and AI to detect and target dust, crumbs, and debris. Equipped with tiny, high-powered brushes and suction mechanisms, they excel at picking up particles from hard-to-reach areas and beneath low-clearance furniture.

Incinerator

This cutting-edge appliance features an advanced combustion chamber that efficiently burns organic waste at high temperatures, reducing it to sterile ash with minimal smoke and odor. Equipped with smart sensors, it automatically adjusts its operation based on the type and amount of waste, optimizing combustion for energy efficiency and safety.

Scrap rats

These intelligent devices are equipped with advanced sensors and AI navigation systems that allow them to traverse the home, efficiently collecting and transporting discarded items from various rooms. With their nimble design, they can access tight spaces and under furniture, ensuring that no trash is left behind.
